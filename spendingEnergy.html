Different Activities Contributing to Energy Expenditure

There are multiple ways through which the human body transforms and dissipates energy.

Some of them include basic bodily functions, while other include additional bodily activities such as walking, working out, etc.

Most of the energy is used up by basic bodily functions, but additional activities are crucial as we can control them. They often make the difference between a healthy and an unhealthy body
