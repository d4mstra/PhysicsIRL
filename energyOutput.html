Energy Output

Energy output, also known as caloric expenditure, refers to the amount of energy that an individual expends through various physiological processes and activities throughout the day.

It encompasses the energy used by the body for basic functions such as breathing, circulation, and maintaining body temperature (basal metabolic rate), as well as the energy expended during physical activities and exercise.
